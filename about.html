Our Story

The Art of the Perfect Donut.

Founded in 2021, Hermit's Donuts started with a simple belief: that a donut should be more than just a treat—it should be an experience.

Every donut we bake is a labor of love. We source our flour from local mills, our fruits from nearby orchards, and our chocolate from ethical suppliers. Our bakers work through the night, hand-glazing each piece to ensure that when it arrives at your door, it's nothing short of perfection.
